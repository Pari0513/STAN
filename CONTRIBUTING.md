# Contributing to STAN

Thank you for your interest in contributing to STAN! This research project welcomes contributions from the community.

## How to Contribute

### Reporting Issues

If you find bugs, have questions, or want to suggest improvements:

1. Check [existing issues](https://github.com/vbrltech/STAN/issues) first
2. Create a new issue with:
   - Clear, descriptive title
   - Detailed description of the problem/suggestion
   - Steps to reproduce (for bugs)
   - Expected vs. actual behavior
   - Environment details (OS, versions, etc.)

### Discussions

For general questions, ideas, or research discussions:
- Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/vbrltech/STAN/discussions)
- Categories: Q&A, Ideas, Show and Tell, General

### Research Contributions

We welcome:

- **Replications**: Testing STAN on different graph datasets
- **Extensions**: Implementing variants (adaptive decay, multi-timeframe stigmergy, etc.)
- **Applications**: Novel use cases (code navigation, citation analysis, etc.)
- **Analysis**: Further statistical validation or theoretical insights
- **Optimizations**: Performance improvements while maintaining algorithm integrity

### Code of Conduct

This project adheres to professional research standards:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on ideas, not individuals
- Welcome diverse perspectives
- Acknowledge prior work appropriately
- Share knowledge openly

## Research Ethics

When contributing:

- Clearly cite sources and prior work
- Distinguish your contributions from existing research
- Use appropriate statistical methods
- Report negative results honestly
- Make data/methods available for replication
